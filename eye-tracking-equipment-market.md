<p><p>Eye tracking equipment can be categorized into two main types: head-mounted and remote viewing systems. Head-mounted eye trackers are worn on the user's head, providing a personal and immersive experience, ideal for applications like virtual reality and gaming. In contrast, remote viewing systems use external cameras to track eye movements from a distance, making them suitable for research and retail environments. Both types enhance user interaction and data collection, catering to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
