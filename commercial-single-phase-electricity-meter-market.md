<p><p>The commercial single-phase electricity meter market includes two primary types: IC card type and non-IC card type. IC card type meters utilize smart cards for prepayment and energy management, allowing users to easily monitor and control power consumption. In contrast, non-IC card type meters operate on a more traditional basis, typically relying on postpaid billing systems without the need for a smart card. Both serve to facilitate electricity usage tracking but differ in payment and management methods.</p></p>
