<p><p>Defatted soya flour is categorized into low denatured and high denatured markets based on protein structure changes during processing. Low denatured soya flour retains a higher proportion of biologically active proteins, making it ideal for nutritional supplements and health-oriented products. In contrast, high denatured soya flour is primarily used in baked goods and processed food products, as it offers better functional properties like water absorption and emulsification. This differentiation influences their applications in various industries, enhancing product performance.</p></p>
