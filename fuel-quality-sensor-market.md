<p><p>The fuel quality sensor market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Optical sensors** detect fuel quality by analyzing light absorption and reflection properties, identifying contaminants.</p><p>2. **Capacitive sensors** measure capacitance changes in fuel, indicating variations in composition.</p><p>3. **Density sensors** assess fuel density, helping to determine quality and composition.</p><p>4. **Acoustic wave sensors** utilize sound waves to gauge fuel properties, detecting impurities.</p><p>5. **Viscosity sensors** measure the resistance of fuel flow, indicating its physical condition and potential quality issues.</p></p>
