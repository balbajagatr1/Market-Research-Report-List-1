<p><p>The medical vacuum plant market consists of two main configurations: single pump and multi-pump systems. A single pump configuration uses one vacuum pump to create and maintain negative pressure, suitable for smaller healthcare facilities or specific applications. In contrast, a multi-pump configuration employs multiple pumps working together to provide higher efficiency, reliability, and redundancy, ideal for larger hospitals or critical care environments where continuous vacuum supply is essential. Each configuration addresses different operational demands and facility sizes.</p></p>
