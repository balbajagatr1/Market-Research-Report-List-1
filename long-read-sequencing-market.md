<p><ul><li>Single-Molecule Real-Time Sequencing (SMRT)</li><li>Nanopore Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The long-read sequencing market primarily includes two advanced technologies: Single-Molecule Real-Time (SMRT) sequencing and Nanopore sequencing. SMRT sequencing utilizes zero-mode waveguides to observe DNA polymerase activity directly, providing high accuracy and longer reads, ideal for complex genomes and epigenetics. Conversely, Nanopore sequencing employs nanopores to analyze DNA or RNA strands as they pass through, offering real-time data and portability, making it suitable for fieldwork and rapid diagnostics. Both methods enhance genomics research significantly.</p></p>
