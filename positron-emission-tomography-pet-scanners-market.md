<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanner market is categorized into full-ring and partial-ring PET scanners. Full-ring PET scanners feature a complete circular detector array, providing enhanced image quality and uniformity. They are commonly used in oncology and neurology for precise diagnostics. In contrast, partial-ring PET scanners have a semi-circular detector configuration, which can be more cost-effective and compact, making them suitable for specific applications or smaller facilities. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing medical imaging and patient care.</p></p>
