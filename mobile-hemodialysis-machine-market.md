<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile hemodialysis machine market is categorized into single pump and double pump machines. Single pump hemodialysis machines are designed for simpler operations, using one pump to manage blood flow, making them cost-effective and easy to transport. In contrast, double pump machines utilize two pumps for enhanced control over blood and dialysate flow, offering improved efficiency and treatment flexibility. Both types cater to the growing demand for portable dialysis solutions, enabling patients to receive care in various settings.</p></p>
