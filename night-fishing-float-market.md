<p><p>In the night fishing float market, there are two primary types: short float and long float. Short float refers to smaller, lightweight floats that are designed for quick, shallow casts, ideal for targeting fish in close proximity. In contrast, long float types are larger and heavier, allowing for deeper water fishing and longer casting distances. Each type serves specific angling needs, with short floats offering precision in shallower areas and long floats providing versatility in various water depths during night fishing.</p></p>
