<p><p>The magnetic chucks market consists of three main types: Ordinary Rectangular Chucks, Strong Magnetic Chucks, and Dense Magnetic Chucks. Ordinary Rectangular Chucks provide basic magnetic holding for lighter workpieces, while Strong Magnetic Chucks offer enhanced holding power, making them suitable for heavy and demanding machining applications. Dense Magnetic Chucks have a tightly packed magnetic configuration, allowing for superior adhesion and precision in complex shapes and thin materials. Each type addresses specific needs in metalworking and machining industries.</p></p>
