<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Air Conditioner market primarily includes two types: Reciprocating Type and Rotary Type. Reciprocating air conditioners utilize pistons to compress refrigerant, providing efficient cooling but often with higher noise levels and complexity. In contrast, Rotary Type air conditioners employ rotating mechanisms that offer smoother operation, compact design, and lower noise, making them increasingly popular in modern commercial vehicles. Both types cater to the need for effective climate control in various commercial vehicle applications.</p></p>
