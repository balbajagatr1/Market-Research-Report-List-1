<p><p>The haptic motors market primarily includes Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs), and other types. ERM actuators create vibrations by spinning an off-center mass, offering simple feedback in devices like mobile phones. LRAs provide more precise tactile responses through linear motion, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed touch sensations, such as wearables and gaming controllers. Other haptic technologies might include piezoelectric and electrostatic actuators, enhancing user experience across various industries.</p></p>
