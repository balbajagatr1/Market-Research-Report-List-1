<p><p>The overhead cranes market comprises primarily two types: single-girder and double-girder cranes. Single-girder overhead cranes feature a single beam, providing a lighter and more cost-effective solution for lifting lighter loads in warehouses and workshops. In contrast, double-girder overhead cranes consist of two beams, allowing for higher lifting capacities and greater stability, making them suitable for heavy industrial applications. Both types are essential for optimizing material handling efficiency in various sectors, including manufacturing and logistics.</p></p>
