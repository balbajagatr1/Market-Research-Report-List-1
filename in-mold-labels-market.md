<p><ul><li>Injection Molding In-Mold Labels</li><li>Blow Molding In-Mold Labels</li><li>Thermoforming IML Processes In-Mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Mold Labels (IML) market consists of three primary types based on molding processes. Injection Molding IML involves embedding labels into plastic during the injection molding process, providing a durable finish. Blow Molding IML integrates labels while forming hollow plastic parts, ideal for containers and bottles. Thermoforming IML utilizes heat to shape plastic and apply labels simultaneously, suitable for packaging and trays. Each type enhances product aesthetics and durability while enabling efficient production methods across various industries.</p></p>
