<p><strong>The Quenching and Heat Treatment Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Oils</li><li>Semi-hot Oils</li><li>Hot Oils</li><li>Bright Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quenching and heat treatment oils are essential in metallurgical processes to control temperature and improve material properties. Cold oils are used at lower temperatures, providing effective cooling for certain metals. Semi-hot oils operate at moderate temperatures, offering balanced cooling and hardening. Hot oils work at elevated temperatures, enhancing thermal conductivity for deeper penetration. Bright oils are specifically formulated for high cleanliness and low residue, ensuring a smooth finish on metal components post-treatment. Each type caters to different processing needs and material requirements.</p></p>
