<p><p>The refractory pan mixer market is segmented into two primary types: dry powder mixing and wet mixing. Dry powder mixing involves combining dry raw materials to achieve a uniform blend, essential for producing refractory products with consistent quality. In contrast, wet mixing incorporates water or additives into the mix, enhancing the material's workability and bonding properties. Both methods cater to different applications in the refractory industry, addressing specific processing needs for storable and moldable materials.</p></p>
