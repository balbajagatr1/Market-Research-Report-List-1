<p><p>The RF modules market can be segmented into three main types: 2.4 GHz, Sub-GHz, and 2.4 GHz / 5 GHz. The 2.4 GHz market primarily supports applications like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, offering higher data rates but limited range due to interference. Sub-GHz modules operate on lower frequencies, enabling longer range and better penetration through obstacles, ideal for IoT and industrial applications. The 2.4 GHz / 5 GHz hybrid combines the benefits of both bands, providing flexibility in performance and connectivity for diverse applications.</p></p>
