<p><p>The Satellite Digital Set-Top Box market encompasses various types, primarily OTT (Over The Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT refers to streaming services delivered via the internet, allowing users to access content without traditional cable or satellite subscriptions. DTT, on the other hand, transmits television signals directly to households using terrestrial broadcast towers. Both types cater to evolving consumer preferences for flexible viewing options, with OTT focusing on on-demand content and DTT providing live television over airwaves.</p></p>
