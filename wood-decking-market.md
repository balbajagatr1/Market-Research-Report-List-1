<p><p>The wood and decking market includes three primary types: traditional wood, wood-plastic composites (WPC), and plastic decking. Traditional wood offers natural beauty and durability, making it a popular choice for outdoor structures. Wood-plastic composites combine recycled wood fibers and plastic, providing resistance to rot and lower maintenance needs. Plastic decking, made entirely of synthetic materials, offers high durability and weather resistance but may lack the aesthetic appeal of natural wood. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
