<p><p>The ePTFE fiber market is categorized into three primary types: membrane, sheet, and others. Sheet ePTFE fibers serve industrial purposes, offering durability and chemical resistance, suitable for gaskets and sealing solutions. The "others" category encompasses diverse applications, including filters, textiles, and composite materials, showcasing the versatile nature of ePTFE fibers in various industries. Each type caters to specific functional needs and performance standards.</p></p>
