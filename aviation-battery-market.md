<p><p>The aviation battery market encompasses three primary types: Nickel Cadmium (NiCd), Lead Acid, and Lithium Ion batteries. NiCd batteries are known for their durability and performance in extreme temperatures, making them suitable for various aircraft applications. Lead Acid batteries, although heavier and less efficient, are cost-effective and widely used for auxiliary power. Lithium Ion batteries are gaining traction due to their lightweight, high energy density, and longer life cycle, offering significant advantages in modern aviation technology.</p></p>
