<p><p>The leak detection equipment market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless leak detection equipment. Wired leak detection systems utilize physical cables and sensors, offering reliable performance and immediate alerts. They are often preferred in permanent installations where connectivity is not an issue. In contrast, wireless leak detection equipment employs sensors that communicate data through wireless networks, providing greater flexibility and easier installation. This is ideal for temporary setups or locations difficult to wire, ensuring comprehensive monitoring of potential leaks.</p></p>
