<p><strong>The Industrial Sprinkler Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Spray Valve</li><li>Radial Spray Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial sprinkler valve market comprises primarily two types: linear spray valves and radial spray valves. Linear spray valves deliver water in a straight line, ensuring targeted coverage for specific areas, which is ideal for narrow spaces. In contrast, radial spray valves distribute water in a circular pattern, providing broader coverage for larger areas. Each valve type caters to different industrial applications, addressing the need for efficient fire suppression and irrigation systems in manufacturing and warehouse settings.</p></p>
