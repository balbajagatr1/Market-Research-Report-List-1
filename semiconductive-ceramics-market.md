<p><ul><li>Two-component</li><li>Multi-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductive ceramics market is categorized into two main types: two-component and multi-component systems. Two-component semiconductive ceramics consist of a primary ceramic matrix combined with a secondary semiconductive material, resulting in enhanced electrical properties and thermal stability. In contrast, multi-component semiconductive ceramics combine three or more components, allowing for greater customization of electrical characteristics, improved performance, and versatility in applications. Both types are essential for advancing electronic devices, sensors, and energy-efficient technologies.</p></p>
